Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver only india or overseas?

We deliver in over 4600 cities within India and to more than 70 Countries worldwide. Our extensive delivery network ensures that your heartfelt gifts reach your loved ones no matter where they are.

Can I choose the delivery time?

Yes, you can choose your preferred delivery time slot during checkout. We offer same-day delivery, next-day delivery, and scheduled delivery options to ensure your gifts arrive exactly when you want them to.

How to Track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order in real-time using our website's tracking feature or through our mobile app. You'll get updates at every step of the delivery process.

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery charges vary based on location and delivery speed. Within India, standard delivery starts from Rs. 99. Same-day delivery may have additional charges. International delivery charges are calculated based on destination country and weight of the package.

What are the different modes of delivery?

We offer multiple delivery modes: Standard Delivery (3-5 business days), Express Delivery (1-2 business days), Same-Day Delivery (within 24 hours), and Midnight Delivery for special surprises. Choose the option that best suits your needs.
